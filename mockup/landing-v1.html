About this place

Nestled on the shores of Seminyak, this architectural masterpiece seamlessly blends Balinese craftsmanship with contemporary luxury. Wake to the sound of waves, then ease into your morning with a dip in the cliff-side infinity pool that appears to pour into the Indian Ocean. The property features four en-suite bedrooms with hand-carved teak furnishings, a professional chef's kitchen, alfresco dining pavilion, and a traditional Balinese garden with bamboo grove. Your dedicated villa manager ensures every detail is attended to.
